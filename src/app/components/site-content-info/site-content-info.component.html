How This Works

The app searches through many of Arizona's vaccination web-portals to find any available appointments.

If any sites with available appointments are found, we showcase their times, and the "Go Register" button will take you to their web-portal to register .

Please make sure to copy the address of the location. Most sites will ask which location would you like to book an appointment. Also have your personal info handy.

If a site without any appointments are found, we make sure to place them at the bottom of the list.
